🎮 How to Play

Welcome to Chance Coin! Pick Heads or Tails and place your stake in BNB.

The game matches you with another player who chose the opposite side and the same stake.

Once matched, the smart contract flips the coin — the winner takes the pot 50/50.

🔗 Connect Your Wallet

To play, connect your wallet (MetaMask or compatible wallets) securely to the platform.

All bets and payouts are handled automatically — no manual transactions required.

💰 Choose Your Stake

Select how much BNB you want to wager. Stakes are flexible, from 0.01 BNB up to your preferred limit.

The smart contract locks in your stake until the game completes.

🎯 Pick Heads or Tails

Make your call and wait for an opponent with the same stake and the opposite choice.

The coin flip is fair, fully on-chain, and transparent.
