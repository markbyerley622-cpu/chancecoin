How to Play

  1. Connect your wallet — link your wallet to get started.
  2. Choose your stake — pick how much BNB you want to play with.
  3. Join a game — lock in your wager and wait for a match.
  4. Pick Heads or Tails — make your call and test your luck.
  5. Wait for an opponent — someone with the same stake and the opposite pick will join automatically.
  6. Win! — the smart contract flips the coin and sends the prize to the winner.
